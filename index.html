The Art Room SA

Join the Art Room founded in 1992 by, artist and writer, Diane Welvering, and have been located in Pretoria East ever since. They work extremely hard to help cultivate the inner artist in all students who visit The Art Room. Using a variety of mediums in our well-equipped studio, we offer workshops and classes to enhance the creative spark and spirit in everyone. Make sure you do not miss out on any of the fun by joining.
